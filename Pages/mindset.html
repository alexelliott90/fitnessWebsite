Mindset

Mental health is incredibly important, in fact it is linked closely with both diet and exercise!

Some of the key things to my mental health are: Sleep, Mindfulness, and Hobbies

Sleep

Sleep is incredibly important to recovery from exercise and to your mental health. I have lost count of the amount of nights that I have had poor sleep due to work stress or other life stresses playing with my mind.

You can improve this with some simple steps

Start by creating a better bedtime routine, and by taking the time to relax before bed by putting away digital devices and doing something unrelated to work. Avoid eating in the two hours before bed, and try to go to bed at the same time each night.

Mindfulness

Mindfulness is important and it ties closely with sleep. Take the time out of your day to be present in the moment, and give your full attention to an activity.

One way to do this is to take part in a hobby - this could be exercise (see the exercise page), or it could be playing a computer game or reading a book. Meditation is also a good way to train your mind in the art of mindfulness. It might take time, but stick with it!

Hobbies

I touched upon this in the previous column, but it deserves one of its own. Make sure that you have some activities that you take part in, that are not related to work. If you are short on time then this is no problem, there are also hobbies that wont take up too much of your time.

You could search the local directories for sports clubs, or take one of your passions and search for one of these. Use online local directories and social media.
