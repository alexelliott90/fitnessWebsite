Nutrition

Nutrition is the second important pillar to building a healthier lifestyle. By combining fitness and nutrition you will improve the effectiveness of your exercise and also ain a healthier mind and body.

Mindset

Your mental health needs equal attention to your physical health! If you are in the right state of mind, you will make your exercise more effective and improve your overall health.

My Fitness Journey

  • At school I was very unfit, and didn't fully understand the importance of exercise
  • At university, I discovered weights, and built muscle whilst combining this with cardio exercise
  • In the years after I left university I continued to build my running distances and reduced my weight exercises, however my nutrition was poor
  • Through improving my diet, and following a proper exercise plan, I was able to improve my 10k run personal best to 43 minutes
  • Following the Covid lockdown, I joined a weights gym and have been combining good weights exercises with outdoor running
  • Key to my lifestyle is the mix of exercise, diet, with the mindset that I can still enjoy treats regularly
  • I also look after my mental health more with meditation and taking time to be mindful
  • In this site, I will share what I think is my most important experience of my journey
